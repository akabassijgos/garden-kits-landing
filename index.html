FAQ & Care tips

Most kits need light, infrequent watering, typically once a week for indoor kits depending on the substrate and temperature. Check the included care card for the specific plant and feel the soil: water when the top 1-2 cm is dry.

Yes, our standard pots are made from biodegradable coir/compostable fibre. They will break down in garden soil over time; check the product description for exact materials.

Many of our kits can be used outdoors. Check the product page for frost tolerance and recommended exposure. For balconies, choose varieties noted as compact and sun-tolerant.

Care tips

  • Place indoor kits near bright, indirect light. Avoid hot direct sun through glass.
  • Start with the watering schedule on the care card, then adjust based on your environment.
  • Use the included biodegradable labels to note planting dates and varieties.
  • Rotate pots occasionally for even light exposure and balanced growth.
